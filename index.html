ğŸš€ Quick Start Guide for Flying Autogyro

  1. Launch QGroundControl on your computer.
  2. Ensure the Pixhawk is properly prepared:
    • Make sure the Pixhawk is powered on,
    • And the Telemetry Radio is securely connected to the Pixhawk.
    • Then either:
      • Connect the Telemetry Radio to your computer, or
      • Use a USB-C cable to connect the Pixhawk directly.
  3. Wait for QGroundControl to establish a connection.
  4. Once connected, read logs to assess sensor status.
  5. Verify sensor health:
    • Check the attitude indicator and GPS panel in QGroundControl.
    • Check all sensor readings in Analyze Tools â†’ Mavlink Inspector.
  6. Once the top-left status reads "Ready to Fly" (in green):
    • Push the throttle stick to the bottom-right corner to arm the aircraft.
  7. Switch to Manual Mode and you're ready to fly!

Purpose: Use your DJI FPV video feed as a source for object detection.

  1. Connect DJI FPV Goggles to your smartphone using appropriate cable.
  2. Launch DJI Fly App on your smartphone and ensure the live feed is visible.

  5. Update cam_num in object_detection_control.py to use the virtual camera (e.g., cam_num=2).
